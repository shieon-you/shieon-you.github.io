OPENS DRM
OPENS (Open EPUB Encryption System) DRM is a technology that encrypts EPUB based eBooks and allows the protected eBooks to be viewed only by authorized users or devices according to licenses. OPENS DRM conforms to international DRM standards (ISO TS 23078-2, ISO TS 23078-3) and industrial standard (Readium LCP*). * ISO/IEC JTC1/SC34 committee is developing the ISO TS 23978-2 based on the Readium LCP technlogy from Readium Foundation.
  • A.   It is a security-proven but fully interoperable DRM technology as an international standard.
  • B.   It is very easy to integrate with existing business system and provides good user experience.
  • C.   With secure delivery function, a vulnerable distribution channel between publishers and service providers can be secured.
  • D.   With expiration period setting, library lending model can be supported.
  • E.   Even though you don't have your own eBook reader, don't worry about it. Standards eBook DRM readers such as R2 Reader from EDRLab can be used freely.
