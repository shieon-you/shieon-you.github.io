License Generation
You can download encrypted eBook from the OPENS DRM cloud server. (The download will be available within 24 hours)
You can also download a license for any encrypted eBook after setting necessary rights information. A generated license whose type is ISO TS 23078-2(Readium LCP) can be opened in the R2 Reader from EDRLab. And a license for ISO TS 23078-3 can be accessed on the OPENS DRM integrated Reader (TDB) The protected eBook must be located at and accessible by specific URL. The URL information should be set in the license.
